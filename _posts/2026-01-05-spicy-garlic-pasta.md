This pasta takes 10 minutes and uses pantry staples. Perfect for busy weeknights!

### Ingredients
* 200g Spaghetti
* 4 cloves of garlic, minced
* 1 tsp Red chili flakes
* Olive oil

### Instructions
1. Boil the pasta in salted water.
2. SautÃ© garlic and chili in olive oil until fragrant.
3. Toss pasta in the oil and serve!
